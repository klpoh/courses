Term Paper Posters

  • 2024/2025 Semester I

Objectives

To provide the opportunity for students to work in small groups to perform integrated modeling and analysis of significant problems concerning sustainability in a selected domain to help decision-makers and stakeholders make clear and defensible decisions. Students are expected to apply combinations of methods covered in the course.

Final Deliverables

  1. Term Paper in report style (PDF or Word)
  2. Computer models and code (Excel, Py, AHP, JPYNB, etc)
  3. A one-page PPT poster ( Template)
  4. A self-recorded 10-minute video (MP4) Presentation
