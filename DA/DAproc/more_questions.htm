Further Questions

 
In the next iteration the analysis may focus on any issues such as:

    Market Success may not be independent of the Change in Price;
    Change in Price may not be independent of the Change in Cost;
    The Average Demand may not be deterministic;
    The solution may not be sensitive to relevance among some uncertainties;
    The information gathering activities may have significunt impact on the Market Success and prices.
     
Answers to those and other questions may provide additional insight into the decision problem.  Any new information is added to the decision basis for another pass through the decision cycle.  If the refinement of the decision problem is no longer warranted, the preferred alternative is selected and implemented.
